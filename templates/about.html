All you need to know about MedBora Hospital

MedBora started about five years ago because of the dire need of good medical personnel in the country.
The hospital is located in Westlands,Nairobi.It has 100 beds and is able to accommodate patients as required.

Do we have the appropriate equipments?

As a way to ensure you have access to good healthcare,we have the best medical equipments and machines.Whereas the surgeons,doctors and nurses skills cannot be put to question.
